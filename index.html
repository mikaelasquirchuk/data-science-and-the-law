DATA SCIENCE + THE LAW:

WHY DOES IT MATTER?

THREE LAYERS OF INTERACTION + HOW DO THEY APPLY?

PRIVACY

Key legislation:

Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)

When does it apply?

"Personal information" = where individual to whom data relates is identified or identifiable

How do I get around it?

Deidentify your information.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Key legislation:

Copyright Act 1968 (Cth)

When does it apply?

"Literary work" includes a table, or compilation, expressed in words, figures or symbols.

How do I get around it?

Make sure you use public data, data you (or your company) own, or get permission.

ANTI-DISCRIMINATION

Key legislation:

Anti-Discrimination Act 1977 (NSW) et al.

When does it apply?

Unfair treatment on the grounds of a person's race, gender, disability status, and any other protected ground.

How do I get around it?

Watch out for subtle discrimination in treatment in your model deployment.
